In this short Life

Instructions

To perform this piece, you need 8 or more singers, each with a separate computer and headphones, in a Zoom call or similar video-conference.
(It really is essential that you use headphones!)

Once you've selected your voice part, click 'Enable Audio.'

When the director signals the start, press 'Start.' You will see a countdown.
When the countdown finishes, you will hear a pitch and see a word.
Sing the word at the given pitch, quite slowly; each word should last 2-5 seconds.

When you've finished the word, press 'Next' and repeat the process.

When you see '[stop]', finish the word you are on. The performance ends when everyone finishes.

The words are from a short poem by Emily Dickinson:
In this short Life That only lasts an hour.
How much - how little - is Within our power.
