In this short Life

Soprano | Alto | Tenor | Bass

Instructions

To perform this piece, you need 8 or more singers, each with a separate computer and headphones, in a Zoom call or similar video-conference.
(It really is essential that you use headphones!)

Select your voice type (you can use this guide to find it).

One member of the group should direct the start of the piece. When directed, press “START”.
You will now see a countdown on screen; at the end of the countdown, you will hear a pitch and see a word.
Sing the word on the pitch you hear, holding it for 3-5 seconds at a medium volume.

When you have finished singing the word, click “NEXT” and repeat the process.

When the screen reads “[STOP]”, finish singing the current word. The piece ends when everyone has stopped.

If you miss the start of the piece (i.e. you don’t press “START” with the rest of your group), it’s not possible to get back in sync. Instead, please sit back and enjoy the performance!

The words are from a short poem by Emily Dickinson:
In this short Life That only lasts an hour.
How much - how little - is Within our power.
