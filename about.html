Everything

This is a 9 minute demonstration of Everything, which will be a 15-25 minute piece for a group of 8+ voices. Understanding of sheet music is not required; each singer receives their pitches and words on a screen. It can either be performed in person or over Zoom or a similar technology.

The pitches are selected from a constantly transforming harmonic field; the words are chosen from a word frequency list, starting from the most common and moving towards the least common.

One singer must coordinate the beginning; otherwise, no director is needed.
