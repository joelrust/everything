Instructions

All participants should be using headphones.

Press the 'Enable Audio' button before starting.

At the director's signal, press START.

When the countdown finishes, you will hear a tone and see a word. Sing the word on the given tone, following the dynamics at the top of the screen.

Sing it slowly, but not too slowly to be understood.

When you are finished, press NEXT and repeat.

When the screen reads STOP, finish the word you are on. When all have stopped, the performance has ended.
