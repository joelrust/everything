Instructions

At the director's signal, press START.

When the countdown finishes, you will hear a tone and see a word.

Sing the word on the given tone, following the dynamics at the top of the screen.

Sing it slowly, but not too slowly to be understood.

When you are finished, press NEXT and repeat.

When the screen reads STOP, finish the word you are on.

When all are finished, the performance has ended.

Setup

Before beginning, place your phone on 'Do not disturb' mode, or turn off the volume for calls and notifications.

Set the media volume so that the tones can be clearly heard, but with as little distortion as possible.

If you end up out of sync (e.g. you lose signal and do not start with the rest of the group), do not panic! Turn off your volume and restart the program. Select your pitches from those you hear being sung around you.

If you are having difficulty hearing the pitches, one earbud may be worn. If more than a third of a group wishes to use an earbud, all should.
