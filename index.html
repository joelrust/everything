Everything

This is a 5 minute demonstration of Everything, which will be a 15-25 minute piece for a group of 8+ voices. Understanding of sheet music is not required; the singers receive their pitches and words on a phone or tablet, with pings that form part of the sonic texture. One singer must coordinate the beginning; otherwise, no conductor is needed.

Groups are encouraged to explore spatial configurations and movement around and/or within audiences.
